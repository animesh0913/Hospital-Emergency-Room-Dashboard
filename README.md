## Project Overview

This project analyzes Emergency Room (ER) performance data to identify operational inefficiencies, patient distribution patterns, and service quality indicators.

The objective of this analysis is to help hospital management improve patient flow, reduce wait times, and enhance overall patient satisfaction.

## Business Problem

Emergency rooms often face high patient inflow, leading to:

- Long waiting times
- Delayed patient attendance
- Uneven department workload
- Resource allocation challenges

This dashboard provides data-driven insights to support operational decision-making.

### 1. Patient Attendance Analysis
- 61% of patients were attended with delay.
- Indicates operational bottlenecks in emergency handling.

**Recommendation:**  
Increase staffing during peak hours and implement improved triage prioritization.
